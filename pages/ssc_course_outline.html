Simulation Source Code

Course Architecture

Three phases · Nine modules · One repeating cycle

Mirrors the transformation path: 1→4→7 · 2→5→8 · 3→6→9

Phase One — Foundation

Establishing coherence · 1 → 4 → 7 · Mental stabilisation

Module 01
The Simulation Framework
Codex position 1 — Perception · Initiation · The First Question
Core question:
"What if reality is a holographic simulation — and you are both its source and its subject?"
Topics: Holographic perception · How the nervous system constructs reality · The purpose of forgetting · The NPC vs the Authentic Self · Why you are here · Numbers as the language of energy
Practice — Life audit: map recurring themes in your life as curriculum, not coincidence. What keeps returning?
Module 02
The Language of Light
Codex position 4 — Structure · Foundation · The Container
Core question:
"If numbers are frequencies, what is your personal code — and how do you read it?"
Topics: 0–9 as stages of energetic evolution · Electric / Magnetic / Aether categories · The Pythagorean system · How to calculate all seven frequencies · Master numbers · Birth date as external circuit · Birth name as internal circuit
Practice — Full blueprint calculation: students receive and sit with their seven numbers and first descriptions before interpretation begins.
Module 03
The Codex — Architecture of Consciousness
Codex position 7 — Inner truth · Self-knowledge · Alignment
Core question:
"What is the map of how consciousness moves — and where are you on it right now?"
Topics: The 3×3 Codex · Mind / Body / Spirit rows · Witness / Actor / Sage columns · The 1→5→9 primary diagonal · The shadow patterns (bypass, fixation, inflation) · The 3–6–9 frame · The infinity loop · Where your numbers sit on the grid
Practice — Codex mapping: place your seven numbers onto the grid. Identify your natural column, your shadow positions, and which positions generate the most friction in your life.
↓ phase 1 complete — coherence established ↓

Phase Two — Embodiment

Activation through lived experience · 2 → 5 → 8 · The body as the instrument

Module 04
Your Blueprint in Detail
Codex position 2 — Duality · Relationship · The mirror of self
Core question:
"Where is the gap between who you are and how you have been conditioned — and what is it costing you?"
Topics: Deep dive into all seven frequencies — light and shadow of each · Life Path vs Expression: the friction between external curriculum and internal frequency · The Triune Trinity · Reading the gaps between your numbers as precise information about your curriculum
Practice — Life review: match five significant past experiences to specific frequency energies. Identify whether you were in the positive or shadow expression — and what the pattern reveals.
Module 05
The Five Lenses — Working With Your Layers
Codex position 5 — The pivot · Embodiment · The incarnation point
Core question:
"Which layer of self are you looking through right now — and can you choose a different one?"
Topics: Ego · Mind · Soul · Spirit · Void — the five lenses and how to recognise each in real time · Where attention goes, energy flows · The body as portal to the present · Emotional intelligence as efficient energy management · The choice-maker and the observer
Practice — Daily lens check: at three points in the day, identify which layer you are operating from. Note which situations pull you into Ego, which open the Soul, and where the choice-maker becomes available.
Module 06
Reading the Pattern — Friction as Data
Codex position 8 — Embodied power · Self-mastery · Will applied
Core question:
"What are your recurring patterns telling you — and how do you use friction as fuel rather than evidence of failure?"
Topics: The shadow patterns of the Codex in lived experience · Triggers as doorways · The choosing point · How to override habitual re-action · Self-responsibility vs self-blame · The simulation as a gym for the will · Situational themes repeat until the lesson is embodied
Practice — Pattern decoding: identify one recurring situation or relationship dynamic. Trace it to its shadow number on the Codex, then identify the medicine — the position you need to develop to move through it.
↓ phase 2 complete — embodiment activated ↓

Phase Three — Integration

Expression, responsibility and release · 3 → 6 → 9 · Living what you know

Module 07
Authentic Expression — Living Your Frequency
Codex position 3 — Creative expression · Meaning · The authentic signal
Core question:
"What does your authentic expression actually look like — and what has been suppressing its signal?"
Topics: Expression number as the authentic channel your nervous system was built to transmit · How conditioning distorts the signal · The gap between the performed self and the authentic self · Synchronicities and angel numbers as feedback from the simulation · The Destined Expression emerging through the friction
Practice — Expression audit: identify three areas of your life where you are living your frequency authentically and three where you are performing. What would shift if you closed that gap by 10%?
Module 08
Integration — Building a Personal Practice
Codex position 6 — Responsibility · Sustainable care · Integration as structure
Core question:
"How do you turn insight into a daily operating system — not a weekend epiphany that fades by Tuesday?"
Topics: The full transformation path 1→4→7→2→5→8→3→6→9 as a repeatable daily process · Building the foundation column (1→4→7) into routine · Sustaining embodiment (2→5→8) through simple physical practice · Energy management in relationships · Boundaries as energetic hygiene · Six as the frame that holds the system
Practice — Design your operating system: using your specific numbers as anchors, build a daily and weekly practice that addresses your foundation column, your embodiment, and your expression. What does your version of the path look like?
Module 09
The Life Calling — Contribution and the Next Cycle
Codex position 9 — Completion · Release · The wisdom transmitted
Core question:
"What is your mission — and how does your unique frequency serve something beyond yourself?"
Topics: The Life Calling number as the synthesis of lesson and gift · The Triune Trinity of purpose · Your blueprint as a gift to others · The continuous optimisation cycle — the spiral, not the circle · Beginning the next revolution at a higher octave · Working with the map as a living system, not a fixed fate
Practice — Mission statement: using your Life Calling, Expression, and Life Path as the three anchors, write a single paragraph that describes what you are here to do. Not a goal. A direction.

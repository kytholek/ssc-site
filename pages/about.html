· The Philosophy ·

Simulation Source Code began with a simple question: what if numerology is not mysticism — what if it is mathematics? Modern physics increasingly suggests that reality is information at its most fundamental level. If this is true, the numerical patterns in your name and birth date are not random — they are parameters set at the moment your simulation was initialised.

· Our Approach ·

We use the Pythagorean system as our foundation, while drawing on deeper esoteric traditions to enrich interpretation. Every reading delivers seven core frequencies, each calculated precisely from your birth data and name. Interpretations are honest, nuanced, and useful — each frequency contains light and shadow, gift and challenge.

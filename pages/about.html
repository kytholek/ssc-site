· Where It Started ·

In my early twenties I got a numerology reading. The numerologist looked at my numbers and told me my life would be filled with heartache and challenge — that I was going to have a hard life. That was it. No context, no direction, no nuance. Just a verdict.

I walked away unsatisfied — but not because I disbelieved numerology. The opposite. Something about the interaction confirmed that the numbers were real and that the reading was wrong. The system had something in it. The interpretation had nothing. That gap is what Simulation Source Code was built to close.

What followed was a decade of immersion — studying the existing numerology canon, doing readings for others, living through enough of my own experience to test every interpretation against reality, and layering in the frameworks that actually explained what I was seeing: simulation theory, consciousness research, and serious inner work. The result is not a refinement of traditional numerology. It is a rebuild.

Some of that personal journey is documented in my book Reality/Lost — the story before the system. Not numerology theory, but what it actually looks like to live through the process of not fitting the life you were handed, and finding your own path from the inside.

· What SSC Actually Is ·

Most numerology hands you a Life Path number and a personality profile. SSC works differently. The first insight that changed everything was recognising that the birth date and the full birth name are not the same kind of information — they encode different things and need to be read as separate circuits.

Birth Date
The External Circuit

The curriculum the simulation has designed for you. The themes, resistances, and lessons life will keep presenting. Your Theme, Life Path, and Achievement are encoded here. This is not what you will become — it is what life will keep asking of you until you embody it.

Full Birth Name
The Internal Circuit

The frequency you are here to express. Your Soul Urge, Outer Persona, and Expression are encoded here. This is the authentic signal beneath your conditioning — what you are built to give back, not what life gives you.

The second insight was structure. Traditional readings often give you a root number and a general description. SSC works with the compound number — the two-digit number before reduction — because the compound tells the specific story of how the energy arrived. A 35/8 Life Path and a 26/8 Life Path are both 8s, but they carry completely different histories. That distinction changes the entire reading.

Seven frequencies. Two circuits. The compound story behind each number. The shadow expression alongside the positive. And at the centre: the Life Calling — the specific directive that emerges when both circuits are run together. That is what a complete SSC reading delivers.

Compound Numbers

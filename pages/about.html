· The Origin ·

Simulation Source Code began after a series of uneffective readings from Numerologists of the time. Continually told "your life will be filled with heartache and challange. You will have a hard life", as anyone would understand, I was not satisfied nor believed in their interpretation. For some reason, I was compelled to find meaning still within the numbers. After a decade of life, experiences, learning and expirimenting, Simulation Source Code Was Born.

· Our Approach ·

We use the Pythagorean system as our foundation, but applying new concepts deriving from experience and learning about simulation theory, consciousness, quantum physics and Spirituality as a whole. Every reading delivers seven core frequencies, each calculated precisely from your birth data and name. Interpretations are practical and useful — offering direction and objectives for growth as opposed to mere dictation.

## Working With Your 5 Life Path

The practical integration of Life Path 5 is not about slowing down — it is about staying long enough to receive what the current experience is offering.

The integration practice: choose one situation you have been partially present in and commit to full presence for a defined period. The Life Path 5 develops its real power not by seeing more of the world but by seeing more deeply into what is directly in front of them.

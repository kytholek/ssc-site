None of that experience happens *out there*. It happens inside your nervous system. The screen is real. But the experience of the screen is constructed within you.

      
This is not "woo-woo" or some radical claim — it is basic neuroscience mixed with quantum physics. What Simulation Source Code does is take it seriously as a framework for understanding everything else, and providing a practical approach to numerology and our simulated life itself.

      
If perception is a four-step process — reception, transduction, translation, projection — then what you call reality is always a projected internal reconstruction of sensory data. But it is being built, continuously and forever, inside you.

The projected reality appears external — but it is an entirely internal translation of data. A mirror for self-correction.

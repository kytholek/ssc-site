How the Simulation Works

Numerology maps every letter and every digit to a number. When those numbers are calculated, reduced, and read together, they form a frequency profile unique to you. SOURCE CODE: LIFE automates all of it — and wraps the results in an RPG interface that makes the meaning genuinely actionable.

01
CREATE YOUR CHARACTER
Sign in and enter your full birth name (as it appears on your birth certificate) and your date of birth. These are the only two inputs the engine needs.
02
CALCULATE FREQUENCIES
The engine computes your 7 core frequencies — Life Path, Expression, Life Calling, Soul, Outer, Achievement, and Theme — using classical numerology methods.
03
READ YOUR JOURNAL
Each frequency unlocks a detailed journal entry covering its meaning — what it is asking you to learn, express, desire, project, accomplish, and ultimately become.
04
ACCEPT YOUR QUESTS
Your frequencies generate a full quest log — a Main Quest (Life Calling), secondary Life Quests, Side Quests, and bonus Abundance Quests. Each includes real-world objectives.

◈ STAT SHEET

Your 7 Core Frequencies

Each frequency is computed from a different slice of your birth data and tells a different part of your story. Together, they form a complete character sheet for the simulation called your life.

LP
LIFE PATH
What you are here to learn. Derived from your full date of birth.
EX
EXPRESSION
What you naturally carry and project. Derived from all letters of your full birth name.
CL
LIFE CALLING
Your primary mission. Derived by combining Expression and Life Path.
SO
SOUL URGE
Your inner desire. Derived from only the vowels in your birth name.
OU
OUTER PERSONA
How others perceive you. Derived from only the consonants in your birth name.
AC
ACHIEVEMENT
How you accomplish. Derived from your birth month and day.
TH
THEME
Your life's overarching curriculum. Derived from your birth year.
◈ GAME FEATURES

What's inside the Simulation

📖
CHARACTER JOURNAL
Seven expandable journal entries — one per frequency — each with a full explanation of your archetype, what the energy means in practice, and a personal affirmation.
⚔
QUEST LOG
A full quest system: Main Quest (Life Calling), secondary Life Quests, Side Quests, and Abundance Quests. Each includes real-world, actionable objectives you can actually complete.
📊
STAT CHARTS
Visual bar charts showing how your numbers distribute across 1–9 from three sources: your 7 core frequencies, your birthdate digits, and your name letter values.
◈
MASTER NUMBERS
11, 22, 33, and 44 are treated as master numbers with their own unique journal entries, quest briefings, and mission objectives. None are reduced away.
⚡
ABUNDANCE QUESTS
Numbers that appear unusually often in your combined code unlock bonus quests automatically. Your specific pattern generates quests no one else will have.
🔒
PRIVACY FIRST
All calculations happen locally in your browser. Your name and birthdate never leave your device unless you create an account. No tracking. No advertising.

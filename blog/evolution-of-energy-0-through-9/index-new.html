The Evolution of Energy: 0 Through 9

March 2025 · The System

Numbers are not symbols invented to count things. They are the stages through which Energy itself evolves — from nothing, into form, through experience, and back again. What follows is that journey, told through each frequency in turn.

0
The Womb of Creation

The Void of Infinite Potential

Zero is the Womb of Creation, symbolised within its very shape — ○. The portal from which life initially emerges and, further down the line, the portal that raises life into higher dimensions.

It is the container from which all other frequencies dance, blend and express themselves in form. It is the closest to the Source, existing simultaneously as nothingness and the amplification of all things. It is the beginning and the end of a cycle.

  • All cycles emerge from nothing
  • All identities dissolve back into nothing
  • The simulation is not the illusion — identity is
1
Initiation of Creation

The Big Bang

One represents the initiation of the Energy of Creation, the orgasmic impregnation within the Womb of Void. The first burst of particulates that are the building blocks for matter. Being the initiator and that which fills and penetrates space, it is the 1st Masculine expression of energy.

In computer vernacular, it is the open path. The open gate for the free flow of energy to create action into existence.

In its current form, the energy is unfocused and unconcentrated. Being so highly charged and of only one polarity, it needs something to bring it all together, to bring balance and efficient use of this power.

  • The Masculine is the electrical charge aspect of energy
  • Initiation without direction
  • Pure potential for action
2
Relationships & Connection

Condensation of Matter

Two represents the coming together of matter into form. It is only with the Feminine aspect (Magnetic) that this solidification of charged particles can come into form. It is the energy that brings together others for the creation of a body in which the Individuated Spark of energy can now abide, with the balance of electrical charge (Masculine) and magnetic cohesion (Feminine).

In the Cosmic timeline, this is the force that leads to the formation of Celestial bodies such as stars, planets and moons. Just as in electricity, the movement of electricity creates the magnetic field, so too in the greater scheme of the Cosmos.

In the Bible, Adam came before Eve. Eve was "made" from Adam. TWO is the effect of the action of ONE. Two is the ripple of the initial splash ONE creates.

  • The Feminine is the Magnetic aspect of energy
  • Connection requires duality
  • Form emerges from relationship
3
Creative Expression

Expression Through Matter

Three is the onset of discovering self-expression through the vessel created by the interaction of 1 and 2. The Masculine and Feminine come together to create the Divine Child of self-expression. With its new found mobility, the embodied energy can now begin to test out action and reaction upon its environment. Creatively expressing itself to see what happens.

In the Cosmic evolution of things, you can see this as the first signs of "life". Though consciousness was present since the beginning, there are now mobile entities for it to embody and express itself.

  • The child of masculine and feminine union
  • First expression of consciousness
  • Playing with creative potential
4
Foundations

Beginning of Organized Structures

At some point, the chaos of a child must be tempered and matured. This needs a foundation of structured growth. Just as a seed needs an optimal environment to truly flourish, so does any being. The Four is that organized structure for the work of growth. The four walls of a house, 4 sides to a door, 4 boundaries to a pasture — four being the number of organized containment for efficiency.

In the cosmic evolution of things, the 4 can be seen as the beginning of smaller "beings" organizing together to create more complex structures or vehicles for consciousness to embody (different specialized cells for organs, etc). Notice now we are in the color green, the FOURTH chakra and color of Earth, life's foundation.

  • Structure enables growth
  • Four directions, four walls, four foundations
  • Organized complexity emerges
5
The Explorer

Discovering Individuality

From the nurturing organized structure offered in the period of 4, there comes a desire to explore outside of our confines. Now imbued with 5 limbs and 5 senses, we have greater mobility and awareness within our environment and are free to explore. Growing tired and bored of its limited environment and same old patterns, the 5 strives for new horizons.

Now come the nomads and explorers. The forging of new paths and discovering new ways to experience life. The release of boundaries is not only physical, but mental. Opening the way to new ways of thinking and innovation.

  • Five senses, five limbs, five directions
  • Freedom from constraint
  • Exploration as growth
6
Parental Guidance

Maturing into Community

After exploring the world and coming into your own expression of self, there comes a time where you find love and a partner to settle down with. Six signifies the family life, community and the guidance and responsibility of a parent to a child.

In the cosmic evolution of things, you can see this as the formation of towns and cities, in which the inhabitants work together to make an easier and more efficient life for all. Does this not sound similar to the Four, when single celled organisms started to come together and work together to create a more complex and efficient way? That fractal pattern just keeps repeating itself.

  • Love brings partnership
  • Family becomes the structure
  • Responsibility as maturation
7
Wisdom Seeker

The Questioning of Self

In the Hindu idea, after your children are grown and you are left with an empty nest, this is the time to pursue knowledge of Self. Following the evolution of the numbers, you have experienced much of what the world offers and are now prepared for the quest of Truth.

Deep sense of knowing and discerning. Research can bring rebellion through knowledge. And ultimate knowledge is the knowledge of self and its ability to reprogram the mental construct and rewire the nervous system to better bring about authentic expression of that Self. The desire now becomes to understand the mind itself, the creator of experience.

In the Cosmic evolution of things, you could see this as the advent of original science — the pursuit of understanding.

  • Inner quest begins
  • Wisdom through introspection
  • Knowledge as liberation
8
Wisdom Embodied

Self-Empowered Knowledge

Through the knowledge gained from its time in the 7, the 8 seeks to apply that knowledge to gain power — either over itself (positive), or others (negative). As the symbol of the Eight alludes to, true power is the balance of the Masculine and Feminine energies within, bridging the higher realms with the lower.

For a time, the energy of the eight will seek to impose its power on others, until it realizes that true power comes only from the influence over itself. Willful application of discipline towards self-control that leads to Self-empowerment.

This is where Spirit truly embodies the flesh, again reflected in the symbol of the 8, where upper realm and lower realm are working and flowing together as one.

  • Knowledge becomes power
  • Balance of masculine and feminine
  • Mastery through discipline
9
Completion & Dissolution

Return to the Void

Nine is the completion of the cycle. The number that holds all others within itself. What begins at 1, flows through 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8, returns to wholeness at 9. It is the wisdom earned, the circle complete, the cycle ready to begin again at a higher octave.

Nine is both ending and beginning. The final note before the return to silence, the death that precedes rebirth. The greatest good dispersed to the many. Completion with grace, release with purpose.

  • All numbers contained within
  • Wisdom made complete
  • Ready for the next octave

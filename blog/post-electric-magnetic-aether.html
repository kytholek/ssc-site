Electric, Magnetic & Aether: The Three Natures of Number

March 2026 · 7 min read

Most numerology treats all nine frequencies as variations of the same kind of thing. A number, a meaning, a trait. But the nine frequencies are not equal in kind. They differ not just in quality but in nature — in the fundamental type of energy they represent.

Simulation Source Code groups the ten digits (0 through 9) into three categories: Electric, Magnetic, and Aetheric. This is not an arbitrary division. It maps directly onto the physics of energy itself and reveals something about the structure of reality that flat lists of number meanings cannot.

Electric Numbers: 1, 3, 5, 7

The odd numbers from 1 through 7 are Electric. Their qualities are active, outward, initiating, and penetrating. They are the frequencies that move into space, that begin things, that push energy into new form.

1 is the initiating impulse — the first burst of energy from the void, the open gate. 3 is expression pushing outward through creative form. 5 is movement, exploration, the force that drives experience into new territory. 7 is the electric reach of awareness toward knowledge, the mind extending itself into investigation.

What unites them is directionality — they all move outward. They create, extend, explore, initiate. They are the masculine principle of energy: not gendered, but directional. The electrical charge that moves into space and demands a response.

Magnetic Numbers: 2, 4, 6, 8

The even numbers from 2 through 8 are Magnetic. Their qualities are receptive, inward, sustaining, and cohesive. They are the frequencies that draw things together, that hold form, that organise the raw force of the Electric numbers into something stable and usable.

2 is the magnetic force that draws the first burst of energy into relationship and form — just as it is the movement of electricity that creates a magnetic field, so Two is the effect of One's action. 4 is the container, the structured walls, the boundaries that allow growth. 6 is the cohesion of integration — care, community, the force that holds a system in responsible relation to itself. 8 is the magnetic mastery of self — the disciplined alignment of inner power.

What unites them is receptivity and organisation. They do not initiate — they respond, consolidate, sustain. They are the feminine principle of energy: not gendered, but cohesive. The magnetic field that draws things into formation.

Electric and Magnetic are not opposites in conflict. They are complementary forces whose interaction creates everything that exists. One without the other produces nothing. Together they produce form.

Aetheric Numbers: 0 and 9

Zero and Nine stand apart. They are neither Electric nor Magnetic — they are Aetheric. And this is where the system becomes philosophically precise.

Zero is the void before initiation. The womb of infinite potential. Not darkness as the absence of light, but the undifferentiated field from which light emerges. It is the stage before the first act, the silence before sound, the space that makes the play of form possible. Every cycle emerges from Zero and, at its end, returns to Zero.

Nine is the void after completion. The cycle has run its course. Everything learned is released back into the field. What makes Nine distinctly Aetheric is its mathematical behaviour: multiply nine by anything, and the result always reduces back to nine. Nine absorbs multiplication without changing. It is the number that contains and completes the entire cycle rather than initiating or sustaining within it.

Zero is the void before initiation. Nine is the void after completion. Different ends of the same quality of stillness — the space before and the space after, the breath in and the breath out.

Why the Asymmetry Matters

The system contains four Electric numbers, four Magnetic numbers, and only two Aetheric numbers. This is not an accident. The symmetry of Electric and Magnetic reflects the balanced dance of active and receptive forces that drives all experience. The smallness of the Aetheric reflects something real: the void — pure potential and pure completion — is encountered less frequently than the active and receptive forces of lived experience. It is the bookend, not the body of the text.

This is also why, in SSC readings, encounters with 0 and 9 carry particular weight. They are not simply another frequency on the same scale as the others. They represent contact with the edges of the cycle itself — the threshold points of emergence and dissolution.

Reading Numbers Through This Lens

Once you understand these three natures, reading any combination of numbers becomes more dimensional. An Electric Life Path (1, 3, 5, or 7) describes someone whose primary lesson is oriented around the outward, initiating qualities of that frequency — movement, expression, investigation, beginning. A Magnetic Life Path (2, 4, 6, or 8) describes someone whose lesson is oriented around reception, structure, integration, and discipline.

When your Electric frequencies and Magnetic frequencies are in tension with each other across your blueprint, the dynamic you feel in daily life — the push toward expression and the pull toward stability, or vice versa — has a precise name and a precise origin.

The Aetheric frequencies (0 and 9) appearing prominently in a reading indicate a life oriented around the threshold experiences: beginnings from nothing, completions into release, encounters with the void between cycles. These are not comfortable frequencies to live at the edge of. They are also, when understood, among the most expansive.

The Current the System Runs On

The deepest implication of this division is not about reading individual numbers. It is about understanding what the simulation itself is doing. The play of Electric and Magnetic forces — active and receptive, initiating and sustaining — is the current that powers the Hologram of Experience. The friction generated between them is the energy source of perception itself.

You are not just living within this system. You are, in some essential sense, one of its frequencies. The specific blend of Electric, Magnetic, and Aetheric energies encoded in your blueprint describes the precise note you contribute to the total field. Understanding that note — its nature, its direction, its relationship to the other notes around it — is what it means to read your own source code.
